How to use Fire Extinguishers

  • Pull off pin
  • Focus nozzle at the base of fire
  • Squeeze lever
  • Discharge cylinder until fire is out

Things to not after use of Fire Extinguishers

  1. Ensure you reecharge and refil immediately after partial or full use
  2. If fire does not go out, pick another fire Extinguisher to continue
  3. If fire goes beyond your capability, call the Fire Service ASAP
  4. Keep safety pin safe as it will be used again after reecharge
